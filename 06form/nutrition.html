Nutrition

The Basics

  • Balance Your Plate: Aim for 50% veggies/fruit, 25% lean protein (fish, beans, chicken), and 25% whole grains (quinoa, brown rice).
  • Pick Quality Carbs: Swap white bread and sugary snacks for complex carbs like oats and sweet potatoes to keep your energy steady.
  • Prioritize Healthy Fats: Focus on unsaturated fats like avocados, nuts, and olive oil while ditching trans fats.
  • Hydrate & Minimize: Drink plenty of water and cut back on the "big three": added sugar, excess salt, and processed foods.

In summary:

Eat less food = Lose weight
Eat less food + move more = Lose weight and build muscle
Eat heltheir food options = Healthier life
